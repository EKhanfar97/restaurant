1. Hummus

    A creamy blend of chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic, served with olive oil and pita bread.

  2. Shawarma

    Thinly sliced marinated meat (chicken, beef, or lamb) wrapped in pita bread with garlic sauce and veggies.

  3. Falafel

    Deep-fried patties made from ground chickpeas, fava beans, and spices, usually served with tahini sauce.

  4. Kebabs

    Grilled skewers of marinated meat (chicken or lamb) with a mix of spices, served with rice or flatbread.

  5. Baba Ganoush

    Roasted eggplant blended with tahini, garlic, lemon juice, and olive oil, served as a dip or spread.

  6. Mansaf

    A traditional Jordanian dish featuring lamb cooked in a yogurt sauce, served with rice and pine nuts.

  7. Kunafa

    A delicious dessert made with thin noodle-like pastry soaked in syrup, often layered with cheese or nuts.
